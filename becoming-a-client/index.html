Becoming a Client

In the first instance I offer all my prospective clients an hour intake session – this gives you, the client, a chance to see how I work and whether the relationship between you and I is one which will be beneficial.
Coaching is a very personal experience and the rapport and relationship between coach and client is crucial, so this initial meeting let’s us both see whether we will have the correct fit.
The topics covered will be information; about you and your life (whatever is comfortable for you at that time) issues that you would like to address, what coaching is and isn’t, and discussing our potential working relationship.  All information shared is completely confidential.
I also hold group intake sessions on various dates, this gives you a chance to hear about the benefits of coaching, with no obligation, on a less personal basis.
