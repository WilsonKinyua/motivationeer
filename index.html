The Zone Club specialises in wellbeing coaching and allowing people to make the most of their minds by getting them in the Zone.

For Individuals:

All those looking to join a positive community on Facebook can head over to The Zone Club FREE Group and join at anytime to find out more of what the Zone Club is all about. We run launches onto the official Zone Club course – Gym Membership for Your Mind – twice a year and so eager members can join a waiting list to be notified of any updates concerning launch dates. The website, that you can easily visit below, has the lessons and videos that will transform the way you take on challenges and give you more self-confidence to achieve your goals. Along with this there are other products such as the Journal, a precious tool to help you organise and track your progress day-to-day. If you want to start your journey and get in the Zone either visit our free facebook group or follow the link to our website and get involved. We can’t wait to hear from you!

For Businesses:

The Zone Club has an extra service for businesses who care about their employees and are keen to improve their wellbeing. There is a correlation between happy staff and higher productivity so see this as an investment, not a cost. These service can be provided bespoke for your business or you can opt for a general “one-size fits all” programme to apply to your business. Please do not hesitate to get in contact and arrange a meeting or call for further information! Show your employees you care, get in the Zone.
