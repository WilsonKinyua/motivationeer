About Us

Prystins Insights Limited Company is a registered incorporation located in Nairobi, in Kenya. The company was born out of a desire to create a better life for people by helping them become a better version of their selves in their business, career and personal finance as God intended them to be.

Many people are not doing what is required for them to become successful, not because they don’t want to, but because they are ignorant of the true biblical principles of success. So, they waste their time, resources and energy doing the wrong things and hoping that what they are doing will make them successful.

This is where Prystins Insights Limited comes in, we want to empower these people and show them the right things to do to lead a better life and become successful.

Prystins Insights Limited aims to always satisfy their clients and make sure every coaching session does leave them the same but makes them better. To achieve this, we are going to employ the right coaching framework with a lot of Biblical principles and use the right coaching tools to help our clients get optimal results.

All our coaching programs will be effective and productive transformational results in our clients because we will make sure we thorough follow-ups and assessments of the progress of our clients. Also, we are going to build a strong team that consists of people who do not only share our vision but are also competent and dedicated to their work.

Prystins Insights Limited Company was established by William Siwa, who has a good understanding of behavior around business , career and personal finance. These behaviors can be modified and improved to become better.

Mr. Siwa brings with him a wealth of over 15 years experience where he has worked with business owners and individuals to build and grow their businesses through effective business and marketing strategies.

Vision Statement/Mission statement

Vision Statement

To be an internationally recognized life coaching company within the next seven years.

Mission Statement

To provide genuine help through our coaching programs in business, career and personal finance and invaluable training and resources through our online hub that will empower people to lead a successful life and become better in all areas of their lives.

Target Market

Our target markets are college students, career personnel and business executives who want to lead a better life and become successful in their endeavors.
