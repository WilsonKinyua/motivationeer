Business coaching

While many coaching companies work with large organisations to help improve productivity, create a culture or tighten up the processes, Prystins insights works with small businesses that are faced with some very different challenges.

There are thousands of small businesses out there that are run by individuals who are trying their best to go it alone (you might even be one of them). As we continue to serve businesses at this size we find that our clients’ business and personal life are one and the same. Cash flow problems and the day to day running of their businesses can negatively impact their health. Meanwhile any problems they have in their personal life may cause them to not be as focused on their business. As a one-person business, any personal issues or shortfalls could directly be affecting their business.

As a small business coach, part of the challenge is figuring out where the issues are and then creating a programme to work through them. Sure, they might help improve the technical aspects of the business, but they also have to make sure that the person running it isn’t burning themselves out too.

Career Coaching

How do you stand out, ask for a pay rise or find your dream job? With a large part of our lives taken up by our work it’s no surprise that career coaching can be a highly profitable coaching niche. Feeling valued at work and being fairly compensated can be life-changing, both financially and in terms of your own feeling of self-worth.

As a career coach we work on building up our clients confidence and helping them carve out their career path. Our advice can be invaluable, helping our clients to figure out when to ask for a raise or when to look elsewhere.

More than just helping them climb the ladder, it’s often about helping our clients who have become stuck in their jobs and feel trapped by their lifestyle. As a career coaches must first get to know our clients to better understand their motivations, what makes them get out of bed each day and what excites them.

Personal Finance Coaching

How Can A Personal Finance Coach Help Me?

A Personal Finance Coach helps you to build the basic building blocks of your relationship with money, and helps you gain a better understanding of it as well. As a Personal Finance Coach we will help to educate you to be able to go away feeling confident and able to fulfil your financial goals.

We are not financial advisers. And we won’t ever sell you a financial product. Instead we will help and work with you to understand what is currently holding you back and help you set ambitious, but achievable goals.

How It Works

Financial Coaching is a collaborative, educational process for individuals and couples looking to improve their financial well-being, establish good financial habits, and achieve financial goals. We meet for 60-minute coaching sessions, by phone, Zoom, Skype, or Google Meet.

Who It's For

Whether you are looking to make real and positive changes to your current financial situation, get some answers to specific questions, or just have someone point you in the right direction, financial coaching may be right for you.

  • Do you make good money but just don’t know where it all goes?
  • Do you want to be more intentional with your money?
  • Does keeping up with your money cause you stress?
  • Are you struggling to achieve financial goals such as saving or paying off debt?
  • Do you feel stuck trying to change certain financial behaviors?
  • Are your beliefs about money sabotaging your finances?
